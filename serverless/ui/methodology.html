The Three-Dimension Framework

Our evaluation is grounded in decades of academic research on the Socratic method. We measure three core dimensions that define effective Socratic pedagogy, each on a 0-10 scale:

Why These Dimensions?

These three dimensions address the most common failures of AI "Socratic" responses:

  • Lecturing instead of questioning: Many AI models default to explaining rather than asking. Brevity forces constraint; Terminal Question ensures inquiry.
  • Leading questions: "Don't you think X is bad?" isn't Socratic‚Äîit signals the correct answer. Directional Socratic measures genuine open-ended inquiry.
  • "Pimping": Clinical educators use "pimping" (humiliating questions to display hierarchy) under the guise of Socratic method. Our framework prevents this by requiring genuine guidance, not gotchas.

This framework is validated by peer-reviewed research from:

  • Clinical Education: Research on Socratic questioning in medicine and law distinguishes genuine inquiry from interrogation
  • Computational Linguistics: Token economy and question detection are tractable with modern LLMs
